# Paper Texture Asset

This file is a placeholder for the paper texture image that should be named:
`paper_texture.png`

You can download a vintage paper texture image and place it here to enhance the app's vintage aesthetic.

Recommended specifications:
- Format: PNG
- Size: 1024x1024 pixels or larger
- Style: Vintage, old paper, parchment
- Opacity: Should work well with 0.05-0.1 opacity overlay

The image will be used as a background overlay in both light and dark themes to create the vintage paper feel throughout the app.
